## 🚀 Strategy 1: Gig Economy / Delivery Driving
**Estimated Earnings: $400–$700/month (part-time)**

Apps like **DoorDash**, **Uber Eats**, and **Instacart** let you start earning within days of signing up.

- **DoorDash:** Avg. ~$14–$17/hr before expenses (~$720/month working part-time)
- **Instacart:** Avg. ~$17–$20/hr, top earners up to $45/hr
- **Uber Eats:** Avg. ~$170–$480/month (part-time)

### ✅ Action Steps:
1. Sign up for **DoorDash**, **Instacart**, or **Uber Eats** today (approval takes 1–5 days)
2. Work **peak hours**: Fri–Sun evenings, lunch hours (11am–2pm)
3. Stack multiple apps simultaneously to maximize income
4. Aim for **3–4 hours/day**, 5 days/week

## 🛍️ Strategy 3: Sell Your Stuff (Declutter for Cash)
**Estimated Earnings: $200–$1,000+ (one-time)**

One of the fastest ways to get cash — sell items you already own!

### Best Platforms:
- **Facebook Marketplace** – Best for local, large items (furniture, electronics)
- **eBay** – Best for collectibles, electronics, brand-name clothing
- **Poshmark / Depop** – Best for clothes, shoes, accessories
- **Craigslist** – Good for furniture, appliances

### Top-Selling Items:
- Electronics (phones, tablets, gaming consoles, laptops)
- Designer/brand-name clothing and shoes
- Furniture and home goods
- Baby/kids items (clothes, toys, gear)
- Sports equipment
- Books, DVDs, video games

### ✅ Action Steps:
1. Walk through your home and collect **20–30 items** to sell
2. Research prices on eBay (sold listings) to price correctly
3. List items with **clear photos in good lighting**
4. Price 15–25% below retail to sell faster
5. Repost/refresh listings every 2–3 days to stay visible

## 💡 Pro Tips to Hit $1,000 Faster

1. **Combine strategies.** Don't rely on just one — mix delivery driving + selling items + one freelance gig.
2. **Work peak hours.** Evenings (5–9pm) and weekends are highest-earning for gig work.
3. **Tell everyone.** Post on social media. Text friends. Word-of-mouth is powerful and free.
4. **Track daily earnings.** Keep a simple note on your phone logging income daily.
5. **Don't wait for perfect.** Start listing, applying, and driving TODAY — momentum is everything.
6. **Reinvest if needed.** If you buy items cheap to resell, even a $5 thrift find can sell for $50 on eBay.
