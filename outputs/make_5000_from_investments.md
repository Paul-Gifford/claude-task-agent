### 🟡 PATH 2 — MODERATE RISK: Swing Trading Individual Stocks
**Best for:** Anyone with $2,500–$4,000
**Timeline:** 1–6 months
**Expected Return:** 20–50% per trade cycle (not guaranteed)

#### How Swing Trading Works:
Swing trading means **buying stocks that are trending up** and selling them after a short move (days to weeks). You're not holding forever — you're riding momentum.

#### Top Stocks to Watch in 2025:
- **NVDA (Nvidia)** – AI chip leader, volatile and active
- **AVGO (Broadcom)** – 106% AI revenue growth, strong momentum
- **TSLA (Tesla)** – High volatility = high swing potential
- **MELI (MercadoLibre)** – Latin American e-commerce growth
- **TSM (Taiwan Semiconductor)** – AI hardware backbone

#### The Swing Trading System:
1. **Identify the trend** — Only trade stocks trending ABOVE their 50-day moving average
2. **Wait for a pullback** — Buy when price dips to support (20–30% below recent high)
3. **Set a Stop-Loss** — Always limit your downside to 5–10% below entry
4. **Set a Profit Target** — Aim for 15–25% gain per trade
5. **Exit and repeat** — Don't get greedy; take profits

#### Example Math:
- Start with **$3,000** → Swing trade NVDA for a +25% gain → **$3,750**
- Reinvest $3,750 → Another +25% gain → **$4,687**
- One more +25% swing → **$5,859** ✅ **(Goal reached in ~3 trades)**

#### Best Free Tools for Swing Trading:

### 🔴 PATH 3 — HIGH RISK / HIGH REWARD: Options Trading
**Best for:** $1,000–$3,000 and those who are willing to learn
**Timeline:** Days to weeks per trade
**Warning:** Options can go to ZERO. Only use money you can afford to lose.

#### What Are Options?
Options are contracts that let you **control 100 shares of stock** for a fraction of the price. If you're right about the direction, gains are amplified 2x–10x. If you're wrong, you can lose 100% of what you put in.

#### Beginner-Friendly Options Strategy: **Buying Call Options**
A **call option** profits when a stock goes UP.

#### Step-by-Step:
1. Choose a strong, trending stock (e.g., NVDA, AAPL, AMZN)
2. Buy a **call option** 30–60 days out (don't buy options expiring in less than 2 weeks)
3. Pick a strike price close to the current price ("at the money")
4. Set a rule: **Sell if it doubles (100% gain) OR sell if you're down 50%**
5. Never risk more than 10–20% of your portfolio on a single option trade

#### Example Math:
- Buy $500 worth of NVDA call options
- Stock rallies 15% → Your options gain ~60–100%
- Sell for $900–$1,000 → Rinse and repeat

#### Best Brokers for Options:
- **Robinhood** – Easy UI, free options trading
- **tastytrade** – Best platform dedicated to options
- **Webull** – Free options trading with good charts

## 🧠 THE MINDSET RULES — Don't Skip This

1. **Never invest money you can't afford to lose** — especially in high-risk paths
2. **Cut losses fast** — A 10% stop-loss prevents a catastrophic 80% loss
3. **Don't chase losses** — If a trade goes wrong, don't double down out of emotion
4. **Take profits** — When you hit your goal, SELL. Don't get greedy
5. **Diversify** — Don't put 100% into one stock or one bet
6. **Stay off social media for tips** — Reddit/TikTok "hot tips" are often pump-and-dump traps
